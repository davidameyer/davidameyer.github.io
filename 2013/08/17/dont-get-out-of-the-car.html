Don't Get Out of the Car

17 Aug 2013

Has this guy ever seen a horror movie?

The FWC says Leon was driving when he and his passenger both spotted about three feet of the snake sticking out of a bush.

He stopped his car, grabbed the snake and started to drag it from the bush.

The FWC said, when the python began to wrap itself around Leon's leg, others were able to kill it with a knife.
